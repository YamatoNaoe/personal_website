  • Oyasumi Punpun

    Punpun Onodera, an idealistic 11-year-old, experiences a shift toward adulthood after meeting Aiko Tanaka and realizing the complexities of relationships. His life takes a darker turn when his father assaults his mother, shattering his idealized views. As Punpun's problems grow, his shy nature transforms into reclusiveness, intensifying his inner turmoil and leading him down a grim path of maturity in this coming-of-age story.

  • Vagabond

    In 16th-century Japan, wild and aggressive Shinmen Takezou and his friend Matahachi enlist in the Toyotomi army seeking glory. After their defeat at the Battle of Sekigahara, the friends are separated. Returning home, Shinmen is falsely accused of Matahachi's murder and left to die. Rescued by monk Takuan Soho, he is given a new identity as Musashi Miyamoto. Vagabond is a fictional retelling of Musashi's journey from a violent swordsman to a wise warrior who learns the value of friendship, self-reflection, and life.
