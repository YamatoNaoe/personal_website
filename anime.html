  • Sousou no Frieren

    After defeating the Demon King, the hero's party disbands, but for the elven mage Frieren, their decade-long journey is only a small part of her thousand-year life. Initially indifferent due to her different sense of time, Frieren slowly realizes how deeply the experience affected her. After losing two of her former comrades, she regrets taking them for granted and vows to better understand humans and form meaningful connections. Though their past adventure has ended, Frieren’s new journey is just beginning.

  • I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

    The detached protagonist, uninterested in others, stumbles upon the secret diary of his lively classmate Sakura, who is terminally ill. Despite her revelation, he remains indifferent, but her persistent optimism leads him to spend her remaining days with her. As their bond grows, Sakura’s carefree spirit gradually disrupts his detached life, opening his heart to the true meaning of living.

  • Steins;Gate

    Eccentric scientist Rintarou Okabe, along with his friends Mayuri and Itaru, creates a "Phone Microwave" that mysteriously turns bananas into green goo. After attending a time travel conference, Okabe discovers the gadget can send messages into the past, leading to unforeseen consequences. As his experiments draw the attention of the secretive organization SERN, Okabe and his friends face increasing danger. Now, he must navigate the chaos caused by his invention, fighting to save both his loved ones and his own sanity.

  • One Punch Man

    Saitama, an unassuming hero who can defeat any enemy with one punch, has grown bored due to the lack of challenging opponents. After catching the attention of Genos, a cyborg who wants to be his disciple, Saitama agrees to join the Hero Association to gain recognition and recapture the thrill of battle. With new allies and enemies, Saitama embarks on a journey to reignite his passion for heroism.

  • Ahiru no Sora

    Sora Kurumatani, despite his short height, refuses to let it hinder his love for basketball. Determined to fulfill his mother's wishes, he joins Kuzuryuu High School’s basketball team, only to discover it's filled with unmotivated punks. Undeterred, Sora challenges them to a match, showcasing his skill and dedication. His relentless passion slowly reignites the team's lost enthusiasm, inspiring them to take basketball seriously once again.
