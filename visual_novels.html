  • House in Fata Morgana

    You awaken in a decaying mansion with no memories, greeted by a Maid who claims you are its Master. She guides you through the mansion’s tragic history, revealing four stories of suffering: siblings torn by envy in 1603, a savage beast in 1707, a businessman consumed by greed in 1869, and a cursed young man and witch in 1099. After witnessing these tragedies, you must decide whether to end your journey or continue, but the question lingers: were you unaffected because they weren't your tragedies?

  • Majikoi

    Kawakami City is known for its samurai spirit, where strength and fighting spirit are highly valued, even in school. Yamato, a second-year student at Kawakami High, shares a close bond with six childhood friends—four boys and three girls. They’ve always stuck together, even having a secret base. As a new semester begins, two new girls join their group, and soon after, things begin to change.

  • Umineko When They Cry

    Umineko no Naku Koro ni takes place in 1986 on October 4 and 5 on a secluded island called Rokkenjima (六軒島). The head of a wealthy family named Ushiromiya Kinzou, who lives on and owns Rokkenjima, is near death, and eleven of his family members arrive on the island to discuss how Kinzou's assets will be divided once he is dead. Also on the island are five of Kinzou's servants and his physician. After the eleven family members arrive, a typhoon traps them on the island and shortly after, people get mysteriously murdered.

  • Kara no Shoujo

    In March 1956, private detective Tokisaka Reiji is hired by a girl named Kuchiki Touko, who asks him to find her "true self." At the same time, a series of gruesome murders involving young women with their wombs removed terrorizes Tokyo. Reiji’s old colleague, Detective Uozumi, enlists his help to investigate. Disguised as a substitute teacher, Reiji infiltrates Ouba Girls Academy, where his sister studies and two students have recently vanished. As the murders escalate, the investigation spirals, revealing ties to similar cases from six years ago. The truth, hidden beneath layers of tragedy, begins to unravel.

  • Summer Pockets

    Summer Pockets takes place on the peaceful, rural island of Torishirojima, with a population of about 2,000. The protagonist, Hairi Takahara, moves to the island to handle his late grandmother's estate after an unpleasant incident in his life. There, he meets four girls: Shiroha Naruse, who forgot her summer vacation; Ao Sorakado, who is chasing island legends; Kamome Kushima, a wealthy girl searching for a pirate ship; and Tsumugi Wenders, a young girl trying to discover herself.
